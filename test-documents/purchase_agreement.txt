ASSET PURCHASE AGREEMENT

This Asset Purchase Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into as of August 22, 2024, by and between GreenTech Innovations Inc., a Texas corporation ("Seller"), and EcoSolutions Holdings LLC, a Delaware limited liability company ("Buyer").

RECITALS

WHEREAS, Seller owns and operates a renewable energy consulting business; and
WHEREAS, Buyer desires to purchase certain assets of Seller's business as described herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements set forth herein, the parties agree as follows:

1. PURCHASE AND SALE OF ASSETS
Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, Seller agrees to sell, transfer, and assign to Buyer, and Buyer agrees to purchase from Seller, the following assets ("Purchased Assets"):

a) All customer contracts and client relationships
b) All intellectual property, including patents, trademarks, and proprietary methodologies
c) All equipment, furniture, and fixtures located at 789 Innovation Drive, Austin, TX 78701
d) All accounts receivable as of the Closing Date
e) All inventory and supplies
f) All permits, licenses, and governmental approvals

2. PURCHASE PRICE
The total purchase price for the Purchased Assets shall be Two Million Five Hundred Thousand Dollars ($2,500,000.00) ("Purchase Price"), payable as follows:
a) One Million Dollars ($1,000,000.00) in cash at Closing
b) One Million Five Hundred Thousand Dollars ($1,500,000.00) in the form of a promissory note bearing interest at 5% per annum, payable in equal monthly installments over five (5) years

3. CLOSING
The closing of the transactions contemplated by this Agreement ("Closing") shall take place on September 30, 2024, at 10:00 AM at the offices of Legal Partners LLP, 123 Main Street, Austin, TX 78701.

4. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES OF SELLER
Seller represents and warrants to Buyer that:
a) Seller has full corporate power and authority to enter into this Agreement
b) The Purchased Assets are free and clear of all liens and encumbrances
c) All customer contracts are in good standing with no material defaults
d) All financial statements provided are true and accurate
e) There are no pending or threatened legal proceedings affecting the business

5. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES OF BUYER
Buyer represents and warrants to Seller that:
a) Buyer has full power and authority to enter into this Agreement
b) Buyer has sufficient funds to complete the purchase
c) The execution and performance of this Agreement will not violate any other agreements

6. COVENANTS
From the date of this Agreement until Closing, Seller covenants to:
a) Operate the business in the ordinary course
b) Maintain all customer relationships
c) Not enter into any material contracts without Buyer's consent
d) Provide Buyer with monthly financial statements

7. INDEMNIFICATION
Seller shall indemnify and hold harmless Buyer from any losses arising from:
a) Breach of Seller's representations and warranties
b) Liabilities not assumed by Buyer
c) Actions taken by Seller prior to Closing

8. NON-COMPETE
For a period of three (3) years following Closing, Seller shall not directly or indirectly compete with the business within the State of Texas.

9. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Texas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first written above.
