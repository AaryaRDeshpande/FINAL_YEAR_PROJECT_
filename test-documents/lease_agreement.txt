RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Lease Agreement ("Lease") is made and entered into on March 1, 2024, between Metropolitan Property Management LLC, a New York limited liability company ("Landlord"), and Emily Rose Davis ("Tenant").

PREMISES: Landlord hereby leases to Tenant the residential property located at 456 Oak Street, Apartment 3B, New York, NY 10001 ("Premises"), consisting of a two-bedroom, one-bathroom apartment with approximately 850 square feet.

TERM: The lease term shall commence on April 1, 2024, and shall expire on March 31, 2025, unless sooner terminated in accordance with the provisions hereof.

RENT: Tenant agrees to pay monthly rent in the amount of Three Thousand Two Hundred Dollars ($3,200.00), due and payable on the first day of each month. Late fees of Fifty Dollars ($50.00) per day shall apply for payments received after the fifth day of the month.

SECURITY DEPOSIT: Tenant has deposited with Landlord the sum of Six Thousand Four Hundred Dollars ($6,400.00) as security for the faithful performance of Tenant's obligations hereunder. Said deposit shall be returned within thirty (30) days after termination of this Lease, less any deductions for damages or unpaid rent.

USE OF PREMISES: The Premises shall be used solely as a private residential dwelling for Tenant and Tenant's immediate family members. No more than three (3) persons shall occupy the Premises without Landlord's written consent.

UTILITIES: Tenant shall be responsible for payment of all utilities, including electricity, gas, water, sewer, trash collection, cable, and internet services.

MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS: Tenant shall maintain the Premises in good condition and shall be responsible for all repairs and maintenance except for structural repairs and repairs to major systems (heating, plumbing, electrical), which shall be Landlord's responsibility.

PETS: No pets are permitted on the Premises without prior written consent of Landlord. If pets are permitted, an additional monthly fee of One Hundred Dollars ($100.00) per pet shall apply.

ALTERATIONS: Tenant shall not make any alterations, improvements, or additions to the Premises without Landlord's prior written consent.

DEFAULT: If Tenant fails to pay rent when due or breaches any other provision of this Lease, Landlord may terminate this Lease upon thirty (30) days written notice and may pursue all available legal remedies.

GOVERNING LAW: This Lease shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York.
